### PerformanceCounter

Result is number of processor ticks after last reset.

### PerformanceFrequency

It's number of processor ticks per one second. 

---

Another method of `uptime` calculation : 

UpTime `=` `PerformanceCounter` `/` `PerformanceFrequency``;`
